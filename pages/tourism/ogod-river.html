OGOD RIVER FIREFLY CRUISE

OGOD RIVER FIREFLY CRUISE

Cruising down the Ogod River, guests make their way to a remote part of town where the thick vegetation attracts thousands of fireflies every night. Surely, it would be a peaceful boat ride, complete with interesting stories on how these insects are connected with the presence of whale sharks that populate the Donsol river every summer.

Small boat: 5 people only Tour Inclusions: Boat, local guide, round trip transfer (from your hotel in Donsol and back) Duration of tour: 1 to 2 hours

Aboard a small boat, you cruise through Ogod River in the dark and stop in front of the trees lining up the river and wait for the fireflies to appear. The tour costs Php 1,250 per boat and is available every day from 5 to 9 pm.

NOTE: The actual firefly watching takes less than 30 minutes; afterward, you’ll be taken back to the registration area. Don’t expect to see an overwhelming amount of fireflies. Some say visibility is better if the moon can hardly be seen because the light from the moon makes the fireflies less visible."

The price of a tour can cost up to P800 plus depending on inclusions and the agency who arranges it.
