PAGURIRAN LAGOON

PAGURIRAN LAGOON

DOM'T MISS: Paguriran’s lagoon is what sets the beach apart. This ring of limestone allows for swimming with colorful fishes and serves as launching pads for cliff-diving. Also, if you’re lucky, on a bright clear day, the Mayon Volcano makes an appearance in the background.

DON'T FORGET: Time your visit during a low tide so you can walk across the long sandbar all the way to the lagoon. Make sure to also come very early to access the lagoon – the water recedes in the morning.

Entrance Fee and Other Charges Entrance Fee: P20 – P50 Camping Fee: P100 (Exclusive of tent) Cottage Rental Fee: P300 (Open Cottage); P500 – P1000 (Cottage)

ACTIVITIES
  • Swimming Beach and Lagoon
  • Low tide walking from the beach to the lagoon
  • Snorkling
  • Camping
  • Bonfire
ADDRESS:

Brgy. Sawangga in the town of Bacon, Paguriran Island, Sorsogon, Philippines
