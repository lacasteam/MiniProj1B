TENTYARD HOTEL

Located in Sorsogon City, Tentyard Hotel - Pangpang is a perfect starting point from which to explore Sorsogon. The hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay. Facilities like 24-hour room service, Wi-Fi in public areas, car park, airport transfer, laundry service are readily available for you to enjoy. Non smoking rooms, air conditioning, desk, fan, television LCD/plasma screen can be found in selected guestrooms. The hotel offers various recreational opportunities. Discover all Sorsogon has to offer by making Tentyard Hotel.
